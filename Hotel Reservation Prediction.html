DATASET SOURCE

Hotel Reservation Dataset

PROBLEM STATEMENT

     Hotel chain just hired me as a junior data scientist. For a long time multiple hotels within the chain faced a problem of surprise cancellations on customers part. This in turn, caused some operational difficulties for the hotel. One of many problems with this was over or under staffing. Due to the outsourcing in the cleaning department number of employees was fluid and always changing based on the number of quests. Large amount of cancellations caused losses in hours paid to the employees without the need for them. Second problem was not enough vacant rooms for newer quests which created certain losses on the Hotels's side. In order to eliminate this problem, Hotel management tasked Data Science department with Lead Data Scientist at the helm to eliminate this problem. Fortunately, data science department was present for some years and been passively collecting data on certain demographics of the consumers. As a junior Data Scientist I was tasked with providing complete ML pipeline and expected to train a model which will be able to classify consumer's willingness to cancel or not.

UNDERLYING ISSUES AND BENEFITS OF SOLUTION

     Surprise cancellations not only affected one branch but losses were incurred across at least 12 branches worldwide. This created substantial financial bleed to the chain. Model creation will cauterize so called financial bleed. As trained ML models are reproducible across most of the data, given the data is mostly intact. Therefore, solution for some of the hotels can potentially be used for all branches in the future, creating a failsafe and a way to continue operations more efficiently.

DATA COLLECTION METHODS

     In order to achieve best results certain quality of input to the model is expected. The system of quest onboarding creates the best conditions for that. Every quest once arrived at the check-in has to provide their identification documents (passport, id, driving license etc.). Reception provides a document which requests consent on using their data in improving the Hotel's operations. If given, data from documents is scanned or rewritten and added to the dataset. Further, data is added according to the stay and options chosen by the quest. Additionally, certain automatic checks are done to ensure if the quest is returning if yes information is in turn added on top of already present data.
